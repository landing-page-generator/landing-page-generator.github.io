Frequently Asked Questions

How often can I use dry shampoo?

You can use dry shampoo as often as needed to refresh your hair between washes. However, we recommend not using it more than twice a week to avoid product buildup.

Is dry shampoo suitable for all hair types?

Yes, our dry shampoo is formulated to work well with all hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and coily hair.

Will dry shampoo leave a white residue?

Our dry shampoo is designed to leave no white residue when used as directed. Be sure to shake the can well and spray from the recommended distance.

Is it safe to use dry shampoo on color-treated hair?

Yes, our dry shampoo is safe for color-treated hair and can help extend the life of your color by reducing the frequency of washes.
