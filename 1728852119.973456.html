About the Course

In today's world, sustainability is no longer a trend; it's a necessity. As designers, we have a responsibility to create beautiful, functional spaces that minimize their environmental impact. This course will empower you with the knowledge and tools to integrate sustainable practices into your projects, creating a positive impact on the planet while advancing your design career.

Course Overview

This comprehensive course covers a wide range of topics, from fundamental sustainable design principles to practical implementation strategies.

Module 1: Sustainable Interior Design Principles

Understand the key principles of sustainable design, including environmental impact assessment, lifecycle analysis, and eco-conscious material selection.

Module 2: Energy-Efficient Design for Interiors

Learn how to optimize energy consumption and integrate renewable energy sources into your design projects.

Module 3: Waste Reduction and Material Selection for Interior Projects

Explore strategies for minimizing waste generation and maximizing material reuse and recycling in your designs.
