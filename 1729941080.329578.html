How to Enable Notifications

1. Visit Google

Open your Google browser or visit any Google service like Gmail, YouTube, or Search.

2. Notification Prompt

You'll see a prompt asking if you want to allow notifications. Click "Allow" or "Yes".

3. You're Done!

That's it! You'll now receive notifications directly from Google.
