About Mentorship Hub

Mentorship Hub is a platform designed to bridge the gap between ambitious high school students and experienced mentors in various fields. We believe that research is a powerful tool for personal and academic growth, and we empower students to embark on exciting projects that showcase their passion and intellectual curiosity.
